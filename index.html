Instructions

  1. Left click on a cell to reveal if it contains a bomb or not.
  2. You score a point for each cell, untill you click on a bomb .
  3. There are 10 bombs in this 10x10 grid (100 cells).
  4. The number in the cell indicates how many bombs are there in adjacent 8 cells of that cell.
  5. If you successfully click all cells without stepping a bomb, You Win!!
  6. If you click on a bomb, the game ends and You Lose!!
  7. Try to score 90 points to win the Game.
